test_list_job_filter()[source]¶

Test the filtering of old jobs when listing.

This test creates 3 test jobs. 2 of them are manipulated directly on the job database (mongodb), so that the updated timestamp is 1 more day than the threshold value for old jobs. The listing is checked for appearance of the jobs. Jobs older than the threshold and being successful are not to show up.
